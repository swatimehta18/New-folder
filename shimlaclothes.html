TRADITIONAL CLOTHES

Pahari Dress

The traditional dress for both men and women in the Himachal Pradesh region, including Shimla, is known as the "Pahari dress..

Himachali Caps

The Himachali cap, also known as the "Kullu cap" or "Pahari topi," is a distinctive feature of traditional attire in Shimla and the surrounding areas.

Shawls

Due to the cold climate in Shimla, shawls are an essential part of traditional clothing. Both men and women wear shawls for warmth and as a fashion statement.

Pherans

In some regions of Himachal Pradesh, including Shimla, people wear a long, loose overcoat called a "pheran" for added warmth during the winter months
